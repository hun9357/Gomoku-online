Rules

These are the game rules you need to know

Basic Rule

Black plays first, and players alternate in placing a stone of their color on an empty spot.

Goal

Each players' goal is to create an unbroken row of five stones horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

Exception

The game ends as a draw either if the players agree on it or if the board is fully covered with stones
